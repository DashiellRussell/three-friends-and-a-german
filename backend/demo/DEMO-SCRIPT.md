# Tessera Demo Call — Pitch Interruption

**Duration:** ~20 seconds
**Context:** Mid-pitch, your phone rings. You pause: "Sorry, one sec — let me grab this."

## The Call

**TESSERA:**
"Hey Dash, sorry to bother you — just a couple quick questions. Do you have a sec?"

**YOU:**
"Yep, keep it quick, I gotta go."

**TESSERA:**
"All good — just three quick ones. How's the headache from yesterday? How'd you sleep last night? And did you take your medication today?"

**YOU:**
"Headache's all gone, don't worry about it. Took some ibuprofen this morning, seemed to sort it out. Sleep wasn't great though — only got about four hours, I was at a hackathon."

**TESSERA:**
"Got it — glad the headache's cleared up. I'll log all that. Good luck with the hackathon — thanks for picking up, Dash!"

**[Call ends.]**
