# Hackathon Reel — 1 Minute Script

## Structure Overview

| Section | Time | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| Hook | 0–3s | Quick grab |
| Why health | 3–15s | Personal motivation |
| Hackathon life | 15–30s | The real experience |
| What we built | 30–42s | Tech stack + product |
| The grind | 42–50s | Building montage |
| Close | 50–57s | Tease + team |
| End card | 57–60s | Follow CTA |

---

## Script

**[0–3s] HOOK — Direct to camera**

> "We went to a hackathon to build something for health — here's what actually happened."

---

**[3–15s] WHY HEALTH — Real, not corporate**

> "All of us have someone in our lives — a parent, a grandparent — who's terrible at keeping on top of their health. Not because they don't care. Because life gets in the way. We wanted to do something about that."

*Visual: Talking head, genuine. Maybe intercut with the team arriving at the venue, signing in, finding a table.*

---

**[15–30s] HACKATHON LIFE — The actual experience, funny + real**

> "But first — you have to survive the hackathon. We'd never all worked together before. The Wi-Fi went down twice. Someone's laptop charger stopped working at midnight. At one point the German disappeared for twenty minutes and came back with a kebab like nothing happened."

*Visual: Real clips — venue chaos, crowded tables, bad Wi-Fi face, charger drama, the kebab moment if you have it, exhaustion setting in. Quick cuts, keep it moving.*

---

**[30–42s] WHAT WE BUILT — Tech + health, fast paced**

> "So what did we actually build? An AI health companion that calls you — like actually rings your phone — checks in on how you're feeling, and flags anything a doctor should know about. We wired up Mistral for the AI brain, ElevenLabs for real-time voice, Twilio so it can literally call your phone, Supabase for the backend, and Next.js to tie it all together. Twelve hours. Four people. A lot of coffee."

*Visual: Quick screen recordings — the app UI, a phone ringing with the AI call, the dashboard showing health trends, code flying by on screen. Text overlays popping in: "Mistral AI" "ElevenLabs" "Twilio" "Supabase" "Next.js" as each is mentioned.*

---

**[42–50s] THE GRIND — Montage energy**

> "Twelve hours of 'does this work?' 'No.' 'What about now?' 'Also no.' 'Wait — wait wait wait — yes.'"

*Visual: Split screens, dark room lit by monitors, empty cans, high fives, head-in-hands, 4am stare, then someone pumping their fist.*

---

**[50–57s] CLOSE — Calm, genuine**

> "We came in as three friends and a German. We left as a team with something we're not done building yet."

---

**[57–60s] END CARD**

> "Follow us to see where it goes."

*Handle. Hold 3 seconds.*

---

## Notes

- **Health is motivation, not product pitch.** "Someone in our lives who's terrible at keeping on top of their health" is relatable and human without being corporate.
- **Tech stack section is fast and visual.** The voiceover names the tools quickly while text overlays reinforce them — viewers don't need to absorb every word, they just need to feel "these people built something real with serious tools." Keep the energy up here.
- **Key product detail to land:** The AI *calls your phone*. That's the "whoa" moment. It's not just another chatbot — it proactively reaches out. Make sure the phone ringing visual is clear.
- **The hackathon section is where personality lives.** Swap in your real disasters — every team has their version of the charger dying and the kebab run. Use yours. The more specific and true, the funnier.
- **The German is a running character.** The kebab line lands because it's so nonchalant. If there's an actual moment like this, use it. If not, swap for whatever the real "classic German move" was.
- **Don't over-polish the hackathon clips.** Shaky phone footage of someone's face lit by a laptop at 3am is better than anything staged.
- **Music:** Light and upbeat through health intro, pick up energy for tech/hackathon chaos, strip back for the close.
- **Comments bait:** People will ask "what did you build?" and "what's the app called?" — that's the whole point. Answer in DMs or tease a future video.

## Tech Stack Quick Reference (for text overlays / B-roll)

| Tech | Role in the app |
|------|----------------|
| Mistral AI | AI brain — extracts health data from conversations, flags critical symptoms |
| ElevenLabs | Real-time voice — natural conversation via WebRTC |
| Twilio | Phone calls — the AI actually rings your phone for check-ins |
| Supabase | Database + auth + file storage (PostgreSQL + pgvector) |
| Next.js + React | Frontend app — dashboard, trends, health logs |
| Express + TypeScript | Backend API server |
| jsPDF | Generates doctor-ready PDF reports |
